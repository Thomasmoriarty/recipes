Doesn't it look yummy!

Materials needed

  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 4 fresh sardines, cleaned and deboned
  • 1 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Handful of fresh arugula leaves

Assembly Instructions:

  1. Toast the whole grain bread slices until golden brown.
  2. Place the fresh sardines on a plate and drizzle them with olive oil and lemon juice. Season with salt, pepper, and dried oregano.
  3. Layer the thinly sliced red onion and tomato slices on one of the toasted bread slices.
  4. Arrange the marinated sardines on top of the vegetables.
  5. Top the sardines with a handful of fresh arugula leaves.
  6. Place the second toasted bread slice on top to form a sandwich.
  7. Press down gently on the sandwich to ensure all ingredients are packed together.
  8. Cut the sandwich in half diagonally and serve immediately.
